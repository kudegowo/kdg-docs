# School Payments

Pay school fees securely through trusted payment providers like Paystack and local banks. **KudEgOwo never holds your money** — we simply connect you to your school's payment system safely.

## How It Works

1. **You pay** — Choose your payment method (card or bank transfer)
2. **We connect** — Your payment goes through our trusted payment partners
3. **School receives** — Funds are transferred directly to your school's account
4. **You get confirmation** — Instant receipt sent to your phone

## Features

### Online Fee Payment

Pay from anywhere, anytime.

- **Debit/Credit Card** — Use your Visa, Mastercard, or Verve card
- **Bank Transfer** — Pay directly from your bank account
- **Naira payments** — Pay in Nigerian Naira (additional currencies coming soon)

### Instant Receipts

No more lost paper receipts.

- Automatic receipt sent to your phone after every payment
- Download receipts anytime from your account
- Share receipts with your employer for reimbursement
- Complete payment history at your fingertips

### Payment Reminders

Never miss a deadline.

- Get notified before fees are due
- View upcoming payment schedules
- Set your own reminder preferences
- Avoid late payment penalties

### Payment Plans

Flexibility when you need it.

- Split large fees into smaller instalments
- Choose a payment schedule that works for you
- Automatic payments on your chosen dates
- No hidden fees or interest charges

### Payment History

Complete visibility into your payments.

- View all past payments in one place
- Filter by child, school, or date
- Download statements for your records
- Track outstanding balances

## For Schools

### Simplified Fee Collection

- Accept payments online without handling cash
- Real-time visibility into all transactions
- Automatic reconciliation with your accounts
- Reduce administrative burden

### Financial Reporting

- Track revenue in real-time
- Generate reports for any period
- Export data for your accounting system
- Identify outstanding balances quickly

## Payment Security

Your financial information is always protected.

- **PCI-DSS compliant** — We meet the highest security standards for payment processing
- **Encrypted transactions** — All payment data is encrypted in transit and at rest
- **Trusted partners** — We work with established payment providers like Paystack
- **No stored card details** — We don't store your full card information

:::info[Important]

KudEgOwo partners with trusted payment providers to process your payments securely. **We never hold or handle your money directly** — your funds go straight to your school through these verified partners.
:::
