# Security & Privacy

Your data and your family's privacy are our top priorities. Here's how we keep everything safe.

## Our Commitment

We believe you should always know:
- What data we collect
- Why we collect it
- How we protect it
- Who can access it

## Data Protection

### What We Collect

We only collect information needed to provide our services:

| Data Type | Why We Need It |
|-----------|----------------|
| **Your name & contact** | To create your account and communicate with you |
| **Your children's details** | To link them to their school and show relevant information |
| **School information** | To connect you with your child's school |
| **Payment history** | To show your receipts and transaction records |

### What We Don't Collect

- We don't store your full card numbers
- We don't sell your data to advertisers
- We don't share your information without your consent

## Security Measures

### Encryption

All your data is encrypted:
- **In transit** — Protected as it moves between your device and our servers
- **At rest** — Stored securely with encryption

### Secure Authentication

- Password protection for your account
- Secure login process
- Session management to protect your account

### Regular Security Reviews

- We regularly review our security practices
- We work with security experts to identify and fix vulnerabilities
- We stay up to date with the latest security standards

## Privacy

### Your Control

You're in control of your data:
- **View your data** — See what information we have about you
- **Update your information** — Keep your details current
- **Delete your account** — Request deletion of your data

### Who Can See What

| Information | Who Can See It |
|-------------|----------------|
| **Your profile** | Only you and school administrators |
| **Your children's info** | You, your child's school, and teachers you're connected with |
| **Payment history** | Only you and your school's finance team |
| **Messages** | Only you and the person you're messaging |

### Third Parties

We only share your data with:
- **Your child's school** — So they can provide services to you
- **Payment providers** — To process your payments securely
- **Service providers** — Who help us run our platform (under strict agreements)

We never sell your data to advertisers or marketing companies.

## For Children

We take extra care with children's data:

- We collect minimal information about children
- Children's data is only shared with their school and parents/guardians
- We don't show advertising to children
- We comply with child data protection regulations

## Compliance

We follow data protection best practices and comply with relevant regulations:

- **Data protection laws** — We follow applicable data protection regulations
- **Payment security** — Our payment partners are PCI-DSS compliant
- **Regular audits** — We review our practices regularly

## Reporting Issues

If you notice anything concerning about security or privacy:

1. **Contact us immediately** — [Reach out](/docs/contact) as soon as possible
2. **We'll investigate** — Our team will look into the issue
3. **We'll take action** — We'll fix any problems and let you know

## Questions?

Have questions about security or privacy? [Contact us](/docs/contact) — we're happy to explain anything in more detail.
