## Join Our Beta Program

We're currently working with select schools and families to test and improve KudEgOwo. As a beta participant, you'll:

- **Get early access** to new features
- **Shape the product** with your feedback
- **Help build** the future of education technology

### For Parents

If you'd like your child's school to join KudEgOwo:
1. Let us know which school you're interested in
2. We'll reach out to the school
3. Once they're onboarded, you'll be invited to join

### For Schools

If you're a school administrator interested in KudEgOwo:
1. Contact us at schools@kudegowo.com
2. We'll schedule a demo for your team
3. We'll help you get set up and onboard parents

### For Tutors

Our tutor marketplace is coming soon:
1. Contact us at beta@kudegowo.com
2. We'll add you to our tutor waitlist
3. You'll be among the first to join when we launch

## Report an Issue

If you've encountered a problem:

1. **Describe the issue** — What happened? What did you expect to happen?
2. **Include details** — What device are you using? What were you trying to do?
3. **Screenshots help** — If possible, include screenshots of the problem

📧 **Email:** support@kudegowo.com
