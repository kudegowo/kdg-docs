# Education

Stay connected to your child's learning journey. View homework, track grades, and communicate with teachers — all in one place.

## How It Works

1. **Teachers post** — Homework, grades, and updates are added by teachers
2. **You're notified** — Get alerts when new information is available
3. **You view** — See everything in your KudEgOwo app
4. **You engage** — Communicate with teachers and support your child

## Features

### Homework & Assignments

Never miss an assignment again.

- **View all homework** — See assignments from all subjects
- **Due dates** — Know when work needs to be submitted
- **Submission status** — Track if your child has submitted
- **Reminders** — Get notified before deadlines

### Grades & Test Results

Track academic progress in real-time.

- **Instant updates** — See grades as soon as they're posted
- **Subject breakdown** — View performance by subject
- **Progress tracking** — See improvement over time
- **Identify challenges** — Know where your child needs help

### Digital Report Cards

Report cards, modernised.

- **View on your phone** — No more waiting for paper reports
- **Download anytime** — Save and share as PDF
- **Historical records** — Access past report cards
- **Teacher comments** — Read detailed feedback

### Attendance Records

Know if your child is in class.

- **Daily attendance** — See if your child was present
- **Class-by-class** — Track attendance for each subject
- **Absence reasons** — Record why your child was absent
- **Patterns** — Identify attendance trends

### Teacher Communication

Connect directly with educators.

- **Send messages** — Reach out to teachers easily
- **Receive updates** — Get messages from teachers
- **Schedule meetings** — Book parent-teacher conferences
- **Share concerns** — Discuss your child's progress
