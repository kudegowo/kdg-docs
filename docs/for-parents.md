# For Parents & Guardians

As a parent, you want to stay connected to your child's education without the hassle. KudEgOwo puts everything you need in one place — from paying school fees to tracking your child's progress.

## What You Can Do

### 💳 Pay School Fees Easily

No more queuing at the bank or worrying about lost receipts.

- Pay fees online using your card or bank transfer
- Get instant receipts sent to your phone
- Set up payment plans if you need flexibility
- View your complete payment history anytime

### 🔔 Stay Informed

Never miss an important update about your child.

- Receive instant alerts when your child arrives at school
- Get notified about homework assignments and due dates
- See test scores and grades as soon as they're posted
- Receive emergency alerts from the school

### 👨‍👩‍👧 Manage Your Family

Keep all your children's information organised.

- Add profiles for each of your children
- Link to their schools with a simple code
- View each child's attendance, grades, and activities
- Communicate directly with teachers

### 🤖 Get Help Anytime

Have a question? Our AI assistant is here to help.

- Ask questions about your child's school
- Get help navigating the app
- Find answers to common questions instantly

## Coming Soon

We're working on even more features for parents:

- **Child Spending Wallet** — Give your child pocket money with parental controls
- **Tutor Marketplace** — Find and book qualified tutors
- **Digital Report Cards** — View and download report cards anytime
- **Financial Literacy Games** — Help your child learn about money through fun activities

## How Payments Work

Your money is always safe with KudEgOwo. We partner with trusted payment providers like Paystack and local banks to process your payments securely. **We never hold or handle your money directly** — your funds go straight to your school through these verified partners.

[Learn more about how payments work →](/docs/how-it-works/payments)

## Get Started

Ready to join? Here's how:

1. **Sign up** — Create your free account
2. **Add your children** — Enter their details and school information
3. **Link to school** — Use the code provided by your child's school
4. **Start using KudEgOwo** — Pay fees, track progress, and stay connected

:::tip[Beta Access]

We're currently in beta and working with select schools. [Contact us](/docs/contact) to see if your child's school is part of the program.
:::
