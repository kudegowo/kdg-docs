# Future Roadmap

These features are planned for **2027 and beyond**. We're sharing our vision so you know what's coming.

## Child Spending & Wallets

### 💡 Child Spending Wallet

Give your child pocket money with parental controls.

- Load money onto your child's digital wallet
- Set spending limits and categories
- Get notified when your child makes a purchase
- Perfect for school canteen and supplies

### 💡 Canteen Payments

Cashless payments at school.

- Children pay with QR code or NFC
- Parents see what their child buys
- Schools manage canteen inventory
- No more lost lunch money

---

## Education & Learning

### 💡 Homework & Assignments

Track your child's schoolwork.

- View homework assignments from teachers
- See due dates and submission status
- Get reminders before deadlines
- Help your child stay organised

### 💡 Grades & Test Results

Stay on top of academic progress.

- View test scores as soon as they're posted
- Track progress over time
- Identify areas where your child needs help
- Celebrate achievements together

### 💡 Digital Report Cards

Report cards, reimagined.

- View report cards on your phone
- Download and share as needed
- Compare progress across terms
- No more lost paper reports

### 💡 Live Virtual Classes

Learning from anywhere.

- Join live classes with video
- Interactive lessons with teachers
- Record and replay missed classes
- Perfect for remote learning

---

## Tutoring

### 💡 Tutor Marketplace

Find the right tutor for your child.

- Browse qualified tutors by subject
- Read reviews from other parents
- Book lessons directly through the app
- Secure payments for peace of mind

### 💡 Gamified Learning

Make learning fun.

- Interactive quizzes and challenges
- Earn rewards for completing lessons
- Compete with friends on leaderboards
- Track progress through levels

---

## Financial Literacy

### 💡 KudiCoins Rewards

Teach kids about earning and saving.

- Children earn KudiCoins for achievements
- Learn to save towards goals
- Understand the value of money
- Fun, age-appropriate lessons

### 💡 Savings Goals

Help children learn to save.

- Set savings goals together
- Track progress visually
- Celebrate when goals are reached
- Build healthy money habits early

### 💡 Money Lessons

Financial education for kids.

- Interactive lessons about money
- Age-appropriate content
- Learn budgeting basics
- Understand needs vs wants

---

## School Operations

### 💡 School Finance Dashboard

Complete visibility for school administrators.

- Real-time revenue tracking
- Outstanding fee reports
- Financial forecasting
- Export reports for accounting

### 💡 School Safety Ratings

Track and improve school safety.

- Safety scores across 6 categories
- Identify areas for improvement
- Compare with benchmarks
- Build parent confidence
