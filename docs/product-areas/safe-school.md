# SafeSchool

Know your child is safe. SafeSchool gives parents peace of mind with secure pickup verification, real-time attendance tracking, comprehensive safety ratings, and emergency notifications.

## How It Works

1. **Secure pickup codes** — Each child gets a unique daily passphrase for verified pickup
2. **Child arrives** — They check in using a PIN or QR code at the school gate
3. **You're notified** — Instant alert sent to your phone
4. **Child leaves** — Pickup person provides passphrase, you're notified
5. **Complete record** — Full attendance and safety history available anytime

## Features

### 🔐 Secure Pickup Passphrases

Ensure only authorised people can pick up your child.

- **Daily unique codes** — Fresh passphrase generated each day
- **Authorised contacts** — Only approved people can receive codes
- **Verification at gate** — Staff verify passphrase before release
- **Emergency codes** — Special codes for unexpected situations
- **Audit trail** — Complete record of who picked up your child

### 📍 Daily Check-In/Check-Out

Know exactly when your child arrives and leaves.

- **Digital check-in** — Children use a simple PIN or scan a QR code
- **Instant notifications** — Get an alert the moment your child checks in
- **Departure alerts** — Know when your child leaves school
- **Late arrival alerts** — Get notified if your child hasn't arrived by a certain time

### 🔔 Parent Alerts

Stay connected throughout the day.

- **WhatsApp notifications** — Receive alerts on WhatsApp
- **SMS alerts** — Get text messages for important updates
- **App notifications** — Push notifications on your phone
- **Email summaries** — Daily attendance summary if you prefer

### 🚨 Emergency Broadcast

Instant communication during emergencies.

- **School-wide alerts** — Schools can notify all parents instantly
- **Lockdown notifications** — Know immediately if there's an emergency
- **Status updates** — Receive updates as situations develop
- **All-clear messages** — Know when it's safe

### 👥 Visitor Management

Schools can track who enters campus.

- **Digital visitor registration** — All visitors sign in electronically
- **Photo ID capture** — Visual record of all visitors
- **Purpose tracking** — Know why visitors are on campus
- **Visitor logs** — Complete audit trail for security

### ⭐ School Safety Ratings

Comprehensive safety scoring across six categories.

- **Overall safety score** — Single number showing school's safety level
- **Category breakdown** — Detailed scores in each safety area
- **Certification levels** — Excellent, Good, Satisfactory ratings
- **Compliance tracking** — OSHA, fire safety, health department standards
- **Audit history** — When inspections occurred and results

## Safety Categories

SafeSchool tracks safety across six key areas:

| Category | What It Covers | What's Measured |
|----------|----------------|-----------------|
| **Physical Security** | Access control, perimeter safety | Gates, cameras, entry points |
| **Emergency Preparedness** | Plans and drills | Drills per year, response time |
| **Staff Training** | Safety certification | % staff certified, training hours |
| **Facility Safety** | Building maintenance | Inspections passed, hazard reports |
| **Health & Wellness** | Hygiene and health protocols | Cleanliness, first aid readiness |
| **Digital Safety** | Online protection | Cyber safety measures, monitoring |

### Safety Metrics Schools Track

- Emergency drills conducted per year
- Staff safety certification percentage
- Average incident response time
- Safety training hours completed
- Parent satisfaction scores
- Facility inspections passed
