# Coming Soon

These features are currently in development and will be available in **Q3-Q4 2026**.

## School Payments

### 🔜 Pay School Fees Online

Pay your child's school fees from anywhere.

- **Card payments** — Use your debit or credit card
- **Bank transfer** — Pay directly from your bank account
- **Instant receipts** — Get confirmation sent to your phone immediately

### 🔜 Payment Reminders

Never miss a payment deadline.

- Automatic reminders before fees are due
- View upcoming payment schedules
- Track your payment history

### 🔜 Payment Plans

Flexibility when you need it.

- Split large fees into smaller instalments
- Set up automatic recurring payments
- Manage your payment schedule

---

## SafeSchool

### 🔜 Attendance Tracking

Know when your child arrives and leaves school.

- Instant notifications when your child checks in
- Alerts if your child hasn't arrived by a certain time
- View attendance history

### 🔜 Emergency Alerts

Stay informed during emergencies.

- Receive instant alerts from the school
- Get updates during emergency situations
- Know your child is safe

### 🔜 Visitor Management

Schools can track who enters campus.

- Digital visitor registration
- Visitor logs for security
- Alerts for unexpected visitors

---

## Communication

### 🔜 School Announcements

Stay up to date with school news.

- Receive announcements directly on your phone
- View past announcements anytime
- Filter by importance or category

### 🔜 Teacher Messages

Connect directly with your child's teachers.

- Send and receive messages
- Get updates about your child's progress
- Schedule meetings or calls
