# Financial Literacy

Teach your children about money through fun, age-appropriate lessons. KudEgOwo helps kids learn to earn, save, and spend wisely.

## Why Financial Literacy Matters

Children who learn about money early are better prepared for the future. They understand:

- The value of hard work and earning
- Why saving is important
- How to make smart spending decisions
- The difference between needs and wants

## Features

### KudiCoins Rewards

A fun way to learn about earning.

- **Earn KudiCoins** — Children earn virtual coins for achievements
- **Complete challenges** — Finish tasks to earn more
- **Track earnings** — See how much they've earned
- **Learn value** — Understand that rewards come from effort

### Savings Goals

Teach the power of saving.

- **Set goals together** — Help your child choose what to save for
- **Visual progress** — Watch the savings jar fill up
- **Celebrate milestones** — Acknowledge progress along the way
- **Achieve goals** — Experience the satisfaction of reaching a target

### Money Lessons

Interactive learning about finances.

- **Age-appropriate content** — Lessons designed for different ages
- **Fun activities** — Games and quizzes make learning enjoyable
- **Real-world examples** — Connect lessons to everyday life
- **Progress tracking** — See what your child has learned

### Budgeting Basics

Introduce simple budgeting concepts.

- **Needs vs wants** — Learn to prioritise spending
- **Simple budgets** — Create basic spending plans
- **Decision making** — Practice making choices with limited resources
- **Consequences** — Understand the impact of spending decisions

## How It Works

### For Children

1. **Complete activities** — Finish lessons, quizzes, and challenges
2. **Earn KudiCoins** — Get rewarded for learning
3. **Set savings goals** — Choose something to save for
4. **Track progress** — Watch savings grow
5. **Achieve goals** — Celebrate success!

### For Parents

1. **Set up goals** — Help your child choose savings targets
2. **Monitor progress** — See how your child is doing
3. **Encourage learning** — Support their financial education
4. **Celebrate together** — Acknowledge achievements

## Learning Modules

| Module | What Children Learn |
|--------|---------------------|
| **Earning** | How people earn money through work |
| **Saving** | Why saving is important and how to do it |
| **Spending** | Making smart choices with money |
| **Sharing** | The joy of giving and helping others |
| **Planning** | Setting goals and making plans |

## Age-Appropriate Content

### Ages 5-7: Money Basics

- What is money?
- Coins and notes
- Simple saving
- Earning through helping

### Ages 8-10: Building Skills

- Needs vs wants
- Setting savings goals
- Making choices
- Basic budgeting

### Ages 11-13: Growing Knowledge

- Longer-term saving
- Comparing prices
- Understanding value
- Planning ahead
