# Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about KudEgOwo.

## General

### What is KudEgOwo?

KudEgOwo is a platform that connects families, schools, and tutors. We help parents pay school fees, track their children's safety and progress, and stay connected with teachers — all from one app.

### Who is KudEgOwo for?

- **Parents & Guardians** — Pay fees, track your child, communicate with school
- **Schools** — Collect fees, manage attendance, communicate with parents
- **Tutors** — Find students, manage lessons, get paid

### Is KudEgOwo free?

Creating an account is free for parents. Schools and tutors may have different pricing — [contact us](/docs/contact) for details.

### What does "KudEgOwo" mean?

"Kudi" means "money" in Hausa, and "Egowo" means "money" in Igbo. Together, KudEgOwo represents our mission to make financial transactions in education simple and accessible across Nigeria.

## Getting Started

### How do I sign up?

1. Download the KudEgOwo app or visit our website
2. Create an account with your email or phone number
3. Add your children's profiles
4. Link to your child's school using the code they provide

### How do I link to my child's school?

Your child's school will give you a unique code. Enter this code in the app to connect your account to the school.

### What if my child's school isn't on KudEgOwo?

[Contact us](/docs/contact) and let us know which school you'd like to see on KudEgOwo. We're actively onboarding new schools.

## Payments

### How do I pay school fees?

Once your school is set up on KudEgOwo:
1. Open the app and select your child
2. View outstanding fees
3. Choose your payment method (card, bank transfer, or USSD)
4. Complete the payment
5. Receive instant confirmation

### Is my payment information safe?

Yes. We partner with trusted payment providers like Paystack who meet the highest security standards. We never store your full card details. [Learn more about payment security](/docs/how-it-works/payments).

### Does KudEgOwo hold my money?

**No.** KudEgOwo never holds or handles your money directly. Your payment goes through our trusted payment partners straight to your school. We simply provide the connection.

### Can I set up a payment plan?

Payment plans are coming soon (Q4 2026). This will allow you to split large fees into smaller, manageable instalments.

### How do I get a receipt?

Receipts are sent automatically after every payment via SMS, WhatsApp, or app notification. You can also view and download all receipts from your payment history in the app.

## Safety & Attendance

### How does attendance tracking work?

When your child arrives at school, they check in using a PIN or QR code. You receive an instant notification. The same happens when they leave.

### What notifications will I receive?

- When your child arrives at school
- When your child leaves school
- If your child hasn't arrived by a certain time
- Emergency alerts from the school

### Can I choose how I receive notifications?

Yes. You can receive notifications via:
- App push notifications
- WhatsApp messages
- SMS text messages
- Email (for daily summaries)

## Technical

### What devices does KudEgOwo work on?

KudEgOwo works on:
- **Mobile** — iOS and Android smartphones
- **Web** — Any modern web browser

### Do I need internet to use KudEgOwo?

Most features require an internet connection. However, USSD payments can be made without internet access.

### What if I forget my password?

Use the "Forgot Password" option on the login screen. We'll send a reset link to your email or phone.

### How do I update my information?

Go to your profile settings in the app to update your name, contact details, or other information.
