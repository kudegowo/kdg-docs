# For Schools

Running a school involves countless administrative tasks. KudEgOwo helps you streamline operations, improve communication with parents, and keep your students safe — all from one platform.

## What You Can Do

### 💰 Simplify Fee Collection

Say goodbye to cash handling and manual tracking.

- Accept payments online (card, bank transfer, USSD)
- Send automatic payment reminders to parents
- Generate instant receipts for every transaction
- Track outstanding balances in real-time
- Offer flexible payment plans for families who need them

### 🛡️ Keep Students Safe

Comprehensive safety management for your campus.

- **Secure pickup passphrases** — Daily unique codes ensure only authorised people collect children
- **Digital check-in/check-out** — Students use PIN or QR code at gates
- **Instant parent alerts** — Notify parents the moment their child arrives or leaves
- **Visitor management** — Digital registration, photo ID, and purpose tracking
- **Emergency broadcast** — Alert all parents in seconds during emergencies
- **Safety ratings dashboard** — Track your school's safety across 6 categories
- **Staff safety training** — Certifications, workshops, and compliance tracking
- **Complete audit trail** — Full record of all campus activity for compliance

### 📊 Manage Your School

Everything you need to run your school efficiently.

- Student enrolment and records management
- Attendance tracking across all classes
- Staff management and communication
- School-wide announcements and notifications

### 📱 Connect with Parents

Build stronger relationships with families.

- Send updates directly to parents' phones
- Share homework assignments and due dates
- Post grades and test results securely
- Enable two-way communication with teachers

## How It Works

### For Payments

KudEgOwo partners with trusted payment providers like Paystack and local banks. Parents pay through our secure platform, and funds are transferred directly to your school's account. You get real-time visibility into all transactions without handling cash.

### For Safety

Our SafeSchool system provides comprehensive campus safety:

1. **Secure pickup** — Each child gets a daily passphrase; staff verify before release
2. **Digital check-in** — Students use PIN or QR code to log arrival/departure
3. **Parent notifications** — Instant alerts via WhatsApp, SMS, or app
4. **Safety ratings** — Track performance across 6 categories (Physical Security, Emergency Preparedness, Staff Training, Facility Safety, Health & Wellness, Digital Safety)
5. **Compliance** — Meet OSHA, fire safety, and health department standards
