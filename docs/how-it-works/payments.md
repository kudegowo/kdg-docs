# How Payments Work

Your money is always safe with KudEgOwo. Here's exactly how we handle payments.

## Our Approach

:::info[Important]

**KudEgOwo never holds or handles your money directly.** We partner with trusted payment providers to process your payments securely. Your funds go straight to your school through these verified partners.
:::

## Payment Flow

Here's what happens when you make a payment:

```
You → Payment Provider → School
```

1. **You initiate payment** — Choose your payment method in the KudEgOwo app
2. **Payment provider processes** — Paystack, your bank, or another trusted partner handles the transaction
3. **School receives funds** — Money is transferred directly to your school's account
4. **You get confirmation** — Instant receipt sent to your phone

## Security

Your financial information is protected at every step:

### PCI-DSS Compliance

Our payment partners meet the Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard — the highest level of security certification for payment processing.

### Encryption

All payment data is encrypted:
- **In transit** — Data is encrypted as it moves between your device and our servers
- **At rest** — Stored data is encrypted and protected

### No Stored Card Details

We don't store your full card information. Payment details are handled entirely by our payment partners.

### Fraud Protection

Our payment partners use advanced fraud detection to protect your transactions.

## Fees

### For Parents

- **No fees from KudEgOwo** — We don't charge you to make payments
- **Standard payment provider fees may apply** — These are set by Paystack, your bank, etc.
- **Your school sets their own policies** — Some schools may absorb fees, others may pass them on

### For Schools

- **Competitive rates** — Contact us for pricing details
- **No hidden fees** — Clear, transparent pricing
- **Volume discounts** — Available for larger schools

## Receipts & Records

### Instant Receipts

After every payment, you'll receive:
- Confirmation on your phone (SMS, WhatsApp, or app notification)
- Email receipt (if you've provided an email)
- Record in your KudEgOwo payment history

### Payment History

Access your complete payment history anytime:
- View all past payments
- Download receipts
- Filter by child, school, or date
- Export for your records

## Refunds

If you need a refund:

1. **Contact your school** — Refunds are processed by your school, not KudEgOwo
2. **School initiates refund** — Your school will process the refund through our system
3. **Funds returned** — Money is returned to your original payment method
4. **You're notified** — We'll confirm when the refund is complete

Refund timing depends on your payment method and bank.
