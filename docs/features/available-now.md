# Features Available Now

These features are ready for you to use today in our beta program.

## For Everyone

### ✅ Create Your Account

Get started with KudEgOwo in minutes.

- Sign up with your email or phone number
- Secure login with password protection
- Profile management for your personal details

### ✅ Add Your Children

Parents can easily add their children's profiles.

- Create profiles for each child
- Add basic information like name, age, and school
- Link multiple children to your account

### ✅ Link to Your School

Connect with your child's school.

- Use a simple school code to link your account
- View school information and contact details
- Stay connected with school updates

### ✅ AI Assistant

Get help whenever you need it.

- Ask questions about using KudEgOwo
- Get instant answers to common questions
- Available 24/7 to help you navigate the platform
