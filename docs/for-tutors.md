# For Tutors

Whether you're a full-time tutor or teaching on the side, KudEgOwo helps you find students, manage your lessons, and get paid securely.

## What You Can Do

### 👥 Find Students

Connect with families looking for tutoring help.

- Create your tutor profile with your qualifications and subjects
- Get discovered by parents in your area
- Receive booking requests directly through the app
- Build your reputation with reviews and ratings

### 📅 Manage Your Schedule

Keep your tutoring business organised.

- View all your upcoming lessons in one place
- Set your availability and rates
- Send reminders to students before lessons
- Track attendance and lesson history

### 💳 Get Paid Securely

No more chasing payments or handling cash.

- Receive payments directly to your bank account
- Automatic invoicing for each lesson
- Clear payment history and records
- Secure transactions through trusted payment providers

### 📝 Track Student Progress

Help students succeed with organised tracking.

- Assign homework and track completion
- Record notes from each lesson
- Share progress updates with parents
- Set goals and milestones for each student

## How Payments Work

When parents book lessons with you, they pay through KudEgOwo's secure platform. We partner with trusted payment providers like Paystack to process payments, and your earnings are transferred directly to your bank account. **We never hold your money** — it goes straight to you.

[Learn more about how payments work →](/docs/how-it-works/payments)

## Get Started

Ready to grow your tutoring practice?

1. **Sign up** — Create your free tutor account
2. **Build your profile** — Add your qualifications, subjects, and rates
3. **Set your availability** — Let families know when you're available
4. **Start teaching** — Accept bookings and grow your practice
