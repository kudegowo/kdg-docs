# Welcome to KudEgOwo

**KudEgOwo** brings families, schools, and tutors together on one simple platform. Whether you're a parent wanting to stay connected with your child's education, a school looking to streamline operations, or a tutor building your practice — we've got you covered.

## What We Do

We make the everyday tasks of education easier:

- **Pay school fees** securely through trusted payment providers
- **Keep children safe** with secure pickup passphrases, real-time check-in alerts, school safety ratings, and emergency broadcasts
- **Track learning progress** with homework updates, grades, and report cards
- **Connect with tutors** to give your child extra support when needed
- **Teach financial literacy** through fun, age-appropriate lessons

## Who It's For

| You Are | What You Get |
|---------|--------------|
| **Parent or Guardian** | Pay fees easily, track your child's day, stay connected with teachers |
| **School Administrator** | Manage enrolment, collect fees, communicate with families |
| **Teacher** | Track attendance, share homework, send updates to parents |
| **Tutor** | Find students, manage lessons, get paid securely |

## Our Promise

We believe education works best when everyone is connected. KudEgOwo removes the friction between home and school, so you can focus on what matters most — your child's success.
